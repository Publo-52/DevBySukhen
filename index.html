Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services and workflow to help you get a clearer picture before we connect.

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A standard website typically takes 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to launch. More complex projects like e-commerce sites or custom web applications may take 8-12 weeks or longer. I'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial discussion and scope definition.
Yes, I offer several maintenance packages to suit different needs. These can include regular updates, security monitoring, performance optimization, and content updates. Many clients find value in retaining my services post-launch to ensure their site remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally.
My core stack includes HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), React, Node.js, and various CMS platforms like WordPress and Shopify. I'm also proficient with design tools like Figma, Adobe XD, and Photoshop. I stay current with emerging technologies and can recommend the best solutions for your specific project needs.
Collaboration is key to successful projects. I include a set number of revision rounds in my proposals (typically 2-3). We'll use project management tools to track feedback and changes. For larger projects, I schedule regular check-ins to review progress and gather your input at key milestones.
I offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and hourly rates for more open-ended work. After our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed proposal with pricing based on your specific requirements. Most web projects range from $2,000-$10,000 depending on complexity, while branding projects typically start at $1,500.
